UV Varnishes and Coatings

We offer a wide range of UV, EB and Aqueous coatings for a wide array of applications starting from Packaging to Labels to Industrial applications.

A wide range of UV coatings for traditional packaging and label application to specialized low migration UV coatings for FOOD packaging.

Many options to choose for different coating application mechanisms from Roller coaters, Flexo coaters, screen, offset, dry offset. Contact us into choose the correct one for your suitability.

Specialized coating systems like Drip-off texture, Braille, Pearlescent, matt are available.

There is an increasing demand for food safe coatings and we provide different libraries for UV Coatings for Food Packaging and labeling.

The Excure all round varnishes are radical UV varnishes for all varnishing machines, and flexo units

DRIP OFF SYSTEM

Description

Drip Off represents an efficient and eye-catching method to achieve matt/gloss effects or matt/structure effects in a single process with just one coating unit.

The principle: in the last ink duct, a primer is partially used. Out of the coating unit, a high gloss coating is applied full area. Where primer and high gloss coating meet, a repellent effect is realized. Where the primer has been left out, you realize high gloss surfaces. In this way, you can produce fine printing designs without a spot coating plate.

Depending on the primer, application amount and the setting of your printing press (power of UV lamps), you can achieve medium to strong structures which provide a highly visual and haptical difference compared to the high gloss coating. The systems are characterized by a very good scratch and scuff resistance.

To create this you get a nice Drip Off effect with the Excure 90212 as Drip Off primer and the Excure 90007 as high gloss coating, both radical UV varnishes.

Features

  • Both UV varnishes are formulated with low toxic ingredients
  • Low in wet odour and very low odour in the cured print
  • Both UV varnishes are available press ready at optimal viscosity
  • Optimal resistance properties will be obtained 24 hours after printing

Applications

  • Wet offset (EXC90212)
  • Varnishing machine/coaters (EXC90007)
