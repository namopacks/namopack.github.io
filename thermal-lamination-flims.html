Thermal Lamination Films

Thermal Lamination Films have extrusion coated surface with low temperature melting resin, which enables the lamination of film to paper products by heat and pressure. Various surface finishes available add value to the printed surface. These films are available in 18 - 250 microns.

Available Types:
  • BOPP Based Thermal
  • PET Based Thermal

Description

It is BOPP based, one side matte and both side treated thermal laminating ﬁlm. Film has extrusion coated surface with low temp. melting resin, which enables the lamination of ﬁlm to paper products by heat and pressure.

Features

  • Excellent natural matte ﬁnish look
  • Excellent resistance to elongation & tear
  • Yellow tint free appearance of roll
  • Fibre-tear bond strength with paper
  • Excellent bursting during laminated sheet separation
  • Matte surface suitable for further ﬁnishing like UV spot coating

Applications

  • Thermal lamination with all kinds of printed & unprinted paper and paper boards like book covers, posters, magazines, Diaries etc.
