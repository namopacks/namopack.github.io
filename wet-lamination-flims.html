Wet Lamination Films

These lamination films are suitable for conventional wet lamination with dispersion, solvent-based, UV or special hot-melt adhesives. All lamination films are pre-treated on both sides for subsequent converting (hot stamping, embossing, UV spot varnishing, etc.) These films are available in 9 - 21 microns.

Available Types:
  • BOPP Based Thermal
  • PET Based Thermal
